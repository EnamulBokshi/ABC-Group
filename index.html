    Our Mission

  • To perform the highest level of quality construction services for our customers at fair and market competitive prices.
  • To ensure and maintain cordial business relations with our existing and would be clients in the industry strictly adhering to the quality policy of our own and of our clients accordingly.

    About Us

    • A.B.C Group is industrial and commercial Engineering and Contracting firm Founded in 2006. Dammam based A.B.C Group Construction Corporation provides general contracting, construction management and pre-engineered metal building services to a broad range of commercial and industrial clients throughout the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Anchored by loyal employees who are dedicated to providing superior service, and grounded in the philosophy that honesty and integrity are as important as the bottom line, A.B.C Group Construction has enjoyed an impeccable reputation for over 15 years. We have never failed to complete a project and have never had a bond claim. Schedules and budgets are meant to be kept, and we strive every day to do so
